Proceeding

    Flowerpowder: A new ecological corridor for pollinators

    Article - Responsive Cities Symposium

    The following paper discusses the development of a codesign strategy aiming to implement an ecological corridor, with a focus on insect pollinators, using abandoned crops as structural elements.

    Citation: Palazzolo Henkes, Aguirre Such, A, Grasso, S., Marengo, M., Neri, I., Murat, M., Rico, E., 2021. 'Flowerpowder: A new ecological corridor for pollinators', Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalonia, Public Play Space Symposium, Barcelona, 11th-12th November 2021(link)


    About [free] time: matching time and spaces

    Poster - 43rd International Association for Time Use Research Conference

    This poster shows a methodology to link time-use survey data with the needs of space in higly dense cities.

    Citation: Palazzolo Henkes, R, Qatanany, H., Galdys, M., Patil, K., Falcon, L., Neri, I., 2021. 'About [free] time: matching time and spaces', IATUR, 43rd International Association for Time Use Research Conference, Barcelona, 27th-28th-29th October 2021(link)


    Time Uses, Space Design: Adaptable Use Of Spaces Based On Citizens Time Use

    Article - Public Play Space Symposium

    This project establishes a framework for the generation and assignment of flexible spaces in the city according to the constantly changing needs of the citizens.

    Citation: Palazzolo Henkes, R, Qatanany, H., Galdys, M., Patil, K., Falcon, L., Neri, I., 2021. 'Time Uses, Space Design: Adaptable Use Of Spaces Based On Citizens Time Use', Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalonia, Public Play Space Symposium, Barcelona, 14th-15th July 2021(link)
