About Me

My name is Riccardo Palazzolo Henkes and I am an Urban Researcher and Consultant.
I hold a Master in City and Technology from the IAAC - Institute of Advanced Architecture of Catalonia accredited through the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya and an MSc in Urban Management and Development from the IHS - Institute for Housing and Urban Development Studies at the Erasmus University Rotterdam. My research interests are focusing on data, urban regeneration, innovation policies, smart cities, and sustainable development.
